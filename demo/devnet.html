What this does

Creates two wallets on Solana devnet (a public test network). Funds the agent with real devnet USDC from Circle's faucet. The agent then sends USDC to the subcontractor — a real on-chain transaction you can verify on Solana Explorer.
Local version: local.html — runs on a local validator, no faucets needed, instant.
